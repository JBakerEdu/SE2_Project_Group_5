Use Case 6: Review Freelancer

Actor: Employer
Pre-condition: The freelancer has been Hyred by the employer.

Description: After hiring a freelancer, the employer can leave a rating from 1-5. The system updates the freelancer’s profile and recalculates their average rating.

Basic Flow:
	1. The employer visits the freelancer’s profile.
	2. The employer clicks the "Rate" button.
	3. The employer selects a rating (1–5).
	4. The employer confirms the submission.
	5. The system adds the rating to the freelancer’s ratings and recalculates the average rating.
	6. The system displays the freelancer's updated average rating.
