Use Case 4: Hire a Freelancer
Actor: Employer
Pre-condition: The employer is logged in and has found a suitable freelancer.
Description: The employer can hire a freelancer by sending an offer and agreeing to job terms.
Basic Flow:
The employer selects a freelancer and clicks "Hyre."
The employer negotiates terms (rate, duration, schedule) through direct messaging with the freelancer.
The employer confirms the hire, and the freelancer receives a notification.
The freelancer starts the job according to the terms set.
Alternative Flow 1: If the employer does not reach an agreement with the freelancer:
The employer selects a different freelancer or cancels the process.
Post-condition: The freelancer is hired and starts working on the task.
